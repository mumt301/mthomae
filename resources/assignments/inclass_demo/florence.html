Florence + the Machine

English indie rock band formed in 2007, with vocalist Florence Welch, keyboardist Isabella Summers, guitarist Rob Ackroyd, harpist Tom Monger, and other collaborating musicians.

Florence Welch has been compared to other female singers such as Kate Bush, Siouxsie Sioux, PJ Harvey, Tori Amos, and Björk.

Discography

Since its foundation, the group has produced four albums:

  • Lungs (2009)
  • Ceremonials (2011)
  • How Big, How Blue, How Beautiful (2015)
  • High as Hope (2018)

Members

Current members include:

  • Florence Welch – lead vocals, percussion (2007–present)
  • Isabella Summers – keyboards, piano, synthesisers, backing vocals (2007–present)
  • Robert Ackroyd – lead guitar (2007–present)
  • Tom Monger – harp, xylophone, percussion, backing vocals (2007–present)
  • Cyrus Bayandor – bass (2018–present)
  • Aku Orraca-Tetteh – percussion, backing vocals (2018–present)
  • Dionne Douglas – violin, backing vocals (2018–present)
  • Hazel Mills – keyboards, backing vocals (2018–present)
  • Loren Humphrey – drums (2018–present)
